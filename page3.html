All That Jazz


Did you find the video helpful?


In jazz dance there is a continuity of motion that appears elegant, sharp, yet smooth. It mixes a wide range of dances all combined having having the elegance of ballroom, the smoothness of ballet, and the sharpness of social dancing. There are complex moves such as turns, jumps, and kicks and easy basic steps, pivots, shoulder movements, and arm placements.

Fun Facts

  • Jazz dance shoes can cost between $25.00 to $50.00.
  • Contemporary, Broadway, and Fusion are popular styles of jazz dance.
  • International Jazz Day is on April 29th over every year-not to get confused with jazz dance.
